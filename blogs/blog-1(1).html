2024 in Review – DocSetGo’s Journey Toward Smarter Healthcare Access

Introduction:
As 2024 comes to a close, we’re proud to reflect on the progress DocSetGo has made in transforming healthcare appointment booking into a seamless, accessible experience for users across the country.

Platform Enhancements

This year, we introduced a completely redesigned user interface aimed at improving navigation and speed. The result?

  • 30% faster booking times
  • Improved calendar syncing with local clinics
  • New onboarding flow that cuts down first-time user friction by half

Reaching More Communities

Accessibility has been a major focus. We’ve rolled out multi-language support, including Urdu, Bengali, and Tamil—helping more users from diverse communities book care in their own language.

Customer Support & Automation

In 2024, we revamped our customer service experience by integrating smart routing into our live chat system. Users now get to the right help faster—resolving 93% of inquiries within 10 minutes.

Looking Ahead

In 2025, DocSetGo will focus on AI-driven doctor recommendations, real-time appointment suggestions, and deeper integration with local pharmacy networks. Our goal: healthcare that’s not just accessible, but intelligent.
